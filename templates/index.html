Optimal foodcard calculator

The tool is designed to help you to calculate the optimal purchases using a foodcard with 10EUR of balance. The Ouput is the number of items you should buy to spend as close to 10EUR and always above.

Instructions

Input instructions:

Write "Product Price": without quotations, a blank space between them, add the items in different lines, no blank space within product name, point as a decimal separator.
(Introduces more than two products)

Correct examples:
Milk 8.9
Bread 2.5
Tortillapatatas 3.55

Incorrect examples:
Milk 8,5 (comma instead of point)
Bread2.5 (no blank space between product name and price)
Tortilla patatas 8.5 (blank space within product name)

Output interpretation:

Optimal purchase: (Total cost, [('Product1', Price1), ('Product2', Price2), ...])
(It will show top 3 results)

Insert input:




Optimals purchases output:

{{ optimal_purchase1 }}

{{ optimal_purchase2 }}
